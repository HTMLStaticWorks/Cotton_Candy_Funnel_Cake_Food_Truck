Frequently Asked Questions

Do you need a power hookup?

Our truck has a built-in quiet generator that can power everything independently. If the venue requires no generators, we will need access to two separate 20-amp circuits.

What happens if it rains?

Cotton candy melts in high humidity and rain. For outdoor events, we require a covered backup location or tent. In case of severe weather, we offer flexible rescheduling.

How much space does the truck need?

The truck requires a minimum space of 25ft length by 12ft width, with a clearance height of 11ft. We need a flat, paved or solid gravel surface.
