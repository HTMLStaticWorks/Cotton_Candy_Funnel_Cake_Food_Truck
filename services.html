Our Services

We provide luxury dessert experiences tailored to your specific event needs.

The Full Carnival Experience

Our flagship service brings the complete CandyLoop fleet to your doorstep. This isn't just a food delivery; it's a mobile festival. Our fully illuminated, neon-accented truck serves as a vibrant backdrop for any large-scale event. From music festivals to massive corporate retreats, we provide the high-volume output necessary to keep the crowd happy without sacrificing the artisanal quality of our spins and cakes.

When you book the truck, you're getting a team of three professional artisans who manage everything from the power supply to the final cleanup. We bring our full menu of gourmet flavors, including seasonal specials and our famous "Midnight Cloud" signature item.

Artisanal Indoor Cart Setup

Not every event happens outdoors, but every event deserves a touch of magic. Our sleek, custom-built indoor carts are designed to fit seamlessly into luxury venues, hotel ballrooms, and private residences. These setups are whisper-quiet and fume-free, focusing on the elegant presentation of our cotton candy spinning process.

The indoor cart experience is highly customizable, allowing us to match the cart's branding and the colors of the sugar to your specific event theme. It's an intimate, sophisticated way to bring a gourmet carnival vibe to your wedding reception or VIP cocktail hour.

Custom Gifting & Party Favors

Give your guests a piece of the cloud to take home. Our custom gifting service specializes in pre-packaged, long-lasting gourmet cotton candy in elegant, reusable tubs. We offer full branding services, including custom-designed labels that incorporate your event's logo, wedding monogram, or corporate branding.

These are perfect for large-scale corporate gifts, wedding welcome bags, or high-end party favors. We use a proprietary sealing process that keeps the cotton candy fresh and fluffy for weeks, ensuring your guests enjoy the experience long after the event is over.

Transparent Pricing Highlights

Professional Hourly Rates

For most events, we operate on a transparent hourly basis starting at $250 per hour. This rate includes two of our expert artisans and unlimited basic spins for all your guests. We require a two-hour minimum for all bookings to ensure we can provide the full setup and service experience you expect from CandyLoop.

Minimum Booking Requirements

For high-demand time slots, specifically Friday and Saturday nights, we require a $500 minimum booking. This ensures we can dedicate our full resources and the entire illuminated truck to your private event. For smaller weekday events, we offer flexible packages starting at lower entry points.

Gourmet Catering Per Guest

If you prefer a per-guest model for larger public events or corporate buyouts, our packages range from $8 to $15 per guest. This tiered pricing allows you to choose between our standard artisan menu and our premium luxury selections, which include edible gold flakes, seasonal organic fruit infusions, and our signature glowing wands.
