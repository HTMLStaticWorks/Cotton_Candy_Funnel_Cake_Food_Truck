Spreading Joy,
One Cloud at a Time.

Sugar Cloud started in 2022 with a simple mission: to elevate the classic carnival treats we all love into a premium, gourmet experience. We believe that dessert isn't just about taste; it's about the spectacle, the nostalgia, and the aesthetic.

What began as a small pop-up tent has grown into the city's most sought-after luxury dessert truck. Our signature glowing cotton candy and artisanal loaded funnel cakes have been featured at hundreds of weddings, corporate galas, and exclusive VIP events.
